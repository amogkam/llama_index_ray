About

Production RL Summit is a free 1-day virtual event that brings together ML engineers, data scientists, and researchers pioneering the use of reinforcement learning (RL) to solve real-world business problems.

Experts from JP Morgan, Riot Games, Dow, and Siemens will share their experience taking RL projects from idea to production. Hear about the biggest technical and business challenges they faced, how they tackled them, what they learned, and the future of RL at their organizations.

And more than listen, do! At the free RL Summit you’ll have the opportunity to:

  • Learn practical tips and best practices for bringing RL into your AI portfolio.
  • Connect and share with your peers and experts in the RL community.
  • Build your RL skills with a hands-on practical introduction to RL led by Sven Mika, lead developer on the popular open source RL library, RLlib.
