We invest in our people

We’re on an audacious mission to build the future of computing — and to accomplish that mission, we want all Anyscalers to be able to bring their best selves to work. We’re making a number of investments company-wide to create an environment of psychological safety, prevent burnout, and unlock potential through diversity, inclusion, and belonging.

Peer coaching

Every new Anyscaler is assigned a peer mentor to help with onboarding and set you up for success from day one.

Education stipend

All Anyscalers can take advantage of a quarterly education stipend to better themselves at work or pursue personal interests.

Team-building activities

It’s not all about work. We get out of the office weekly for happy hours, game nights, team dinners, and more.

Benefits

Health care

We offer a wide range of health, dental, and vision coverage options for you and your family — including many that are 99% covered by Anyscale.

Commute reimbursement

Whether you’re hopping on public transit or driving in yourself, Anyscale covers a portion of your commuting costs each month.

In-office meals

Lunch is served every day in our San Francisco office. Dinner, too, if you’re ever working late. And did we mention daily boba runs?

Paid volunteer time off

Give back to the causes and communities you love with paid time off specifically for volunteer work.

Paid parental leave

Anyscalers can take advantage of 12 weeks of paid leave following the arrival of a new little one.

Flexible Time Off

Paid time off at Anyscale is flexible and unlimited. We encourage all Anyscalers to rest up and recharge when needed.
